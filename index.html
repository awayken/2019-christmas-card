The Rauschword Puzzle

Welcome to our 2019 Christmas Card: The Rauschword Puzzle!

We've taken some key facts about our year and built them into an interactive crossword puzzle. 2019 was quite a year for us. I'd tell you about it, but that would give you an edge on the puzzle. You won't trick me, cheater!

The Blue square is your active square. The Yellow squares are your active clue. Fill in all the White squares to complete the puzzle.

If you have a keyboarded device, like a laptop or desktop computer, you can use the Delete Key to empty a square and the Spacebar to switch directions.
